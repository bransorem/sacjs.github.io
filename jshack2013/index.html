Sacramento JavaScript Meetup

JSHack 2013

SacJS is proud to announce the first JavaScript hack for Sacramento. The concept is simple: Create a JavaScript from scratch in 24 hours.

When
Friday June 14 2013 at 6:00pm — Saturday June 15 2013 at 7:00pm
Where
Hacker Lab
Who
Coders and Designers

Official Rules

  • Projects must be started from scratch starting at 6pm on Friday
  • Teams may be from 1-6 people
  • Use of front-end frameworks are encouraged
  • Any server-side platform may be used
  • You may only work 24 hours, we present starting at 6pm on Saturday
